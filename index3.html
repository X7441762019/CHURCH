OUR CHURCH


Modern SDA Church is a branch of the universal Seventh Day Adventist Movement. Our church has many programmes tailored for the spiritual growth of our members. Apart from inhouse ministry we also have outreach programmes like crusades and visitations to members of our community. Our church is currently part of the CRVC (Central Rift Valley Conference) which is a division of the general conference.

Our Leadership

Our church is comprised of various ministries each led by various leaders. Ministries include the Modern sda Children Ministry, Adventist Men, Women Ministry and the Youth Ministry. Our church is currently led by Pst. Pius Kithuku who is also the Lanet District Pastor.
